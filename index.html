Daniel King Hi there, I'm Daniel.

My career began as a technical writer in the boom of ecommerce. I launched help.shopify.com in 2013 as part of a lean team of two others. Shopify's admiration for entrepreneurship made me want to try it for myself.


In 2017, I co-founded getonly.ca. Today, I act as the CTO and we operate with a lightweight tech stack. Offline, I am also a property manager.


Looking to increase my technical abilities, in 2022 I joined the community studying full-stack JavaScript through The Odin Project. Now I specialize in building things from scratch, or with frameworks, or on Shopify. I enjoy solving new problems in creative ways, and old problems with available resources.


I've shared some of my work on this site... feel free to ask me about it if it interests you. Thanks for stopping by!
