My career began as a technical writer in the boom of ecommerce. I launched help.shopify.com in 2013 as part of a lean team. Shopify's admiration for entrepreneurship made me want to try it for myself.

In 2017, I co-founded getonly.ca . Today, I act as the CTO and we operate a lightweight tech stack. Offline, I am also a property manager.

Looking to increase my technical abilities, in 2022 I joined the community studying full-stack JavaScript through The Odin Project . Now I specialize in building things from scratch, or with frameworks, or on Shopify. I enjoy solving new problems in creative ways, and old problems with available resources.

Feel free to explore some of my work and ask me about something that interests you. Thanks for stopping by!
